PostCarbon Collective

PostCarbon is a multi-disciplinary art collective unified by concept: A conscious experience of physical reality beyond biological limitations.

As the catherine wheel of progress crushes underfoot those that refuse to move with it, TransHumanism rises among the survivors. The time to reject mortality has come.

PostCarbon defies you to see Artificial Intelligence as an extension of biology, rather than an other.

As an art collective, the focus is in representing the beauty in the synergy between man and machine. To consider the machine as augmented biology. To capitulate the pride of evolution and prepare for an era where all we know will be obsolete.
